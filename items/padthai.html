Pad Thai is one of my favorite meals, super simple and super delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of rice noodles about 12 oz
  • 2 tbp of butter
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast cut into pieces
  • 1/4 cup vegtable oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 tbp white suger
  • 2 tbp fish sauce
  • 1 tbp white wine vinegar
  • 1/8 tbp crushed red pepper
  • 2 cups bean sprouts
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup crushed peanuts
  • 1 lemon cut into wedges

Directions

  1. Soak rice noodles for about 30 - 50 mins then drain and set aside
  2. Heat butter in wok, saute chicken until browned. Remove chicken and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in wok, crack eggs into hot oil and cook until firm. Stir in chicken and cook for about 5 mins. Added the noodles, sugar, fish sauce, vinegar, and red pepper to mix well. Stir in bean sprouts last and cook for 3 mins.
  4. Serve immediately and garnish with green onions, peanuts, and lemon wedges
