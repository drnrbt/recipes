Broccoli Cheddar soup is one of my favorite meals to have in cold weather

Ingredients

  • 3 cans of chicken broth
  • 3 packages frozen chopped broccoli
  • 6 tbp margarine
  • 1 med onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 1/2 pounds velveeta or similar, cubed
  • 1 pinch ground white pepper

Directions

  1. Bring broth to simmer over medium heat. Cook broccoli in simmering broth for 15 mins
  2. Melt margarine in separate skillet over medium heat, adding onions and sauteeing for 5 mins. Stir in flour until pasty, gradually add milk and stir until thick
  3. Add onions to broccoli mixture in the pot and stir well. Stir in cheese until melted. Do not boil. Season and finish with white pepper
